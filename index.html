Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear?

We recommend cocktail attire for the reception. Given the mountain setting, please bring a light jacket as evenings can be cool.

Will transportation be provided?

We will provide shuttle service from the Four Seasons Resort to the venue and back. Details will be shared closer to the wedding date.

Can I bring a plus one?

Plus ones are indicated on your invitation. If you have questions about your invitation, please contact us directly.

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive 15-30 minutes before the ceremony start time to allow for seating and parking.

Will the ceremony be outdoors?

Yes, both the ceremony and reception will be outdoors. We have backup indoor options in case of inclement weather.
